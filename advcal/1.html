Trippy and Maverick

Trippy and Maverick, two horses so in love
They trotted and galloped under skies above
Together they went on adventures each day
Their bond grew stronger in every way

They took a holiday to the beach one day
The warm sun on their backs, the breeze in their manes
They galloped along the shore, so full of glee
Exploring all that the beach had to see

In the water they played, in the sand they rolled
So happy and carefree, their hearts full and bold
As the sun began to set, they lay on the shore
Watching the stars come out, wanting the day to be more

But eventually, they knew they must go
Back to the stable, their home sweet and low
But their memories of the beach, they would always keep
A special holiday together, forever to sleep.
