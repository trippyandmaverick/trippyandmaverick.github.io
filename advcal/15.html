Maverick: "Speakin' of duties, I think it's my duty to order another round of beers for the table. We deserve a cold one after all we've been through. It's been a real horse of a journey."

Wise Old Dragon: "Indeed you have. But remember, with great adventure comes great responsibility. Don't let your curiosity get the best of you again. Maverick: "Don't worry, we've learned our lesson. We'll be more careful from now on. We don't want to be horse criminals. We'll make sure to think before we act, and make sure we don't cause any more destruction in the galaxy."
