19. Dezember

Trippy: Hey, Maverick, do you think we’ll encounter any dangerous creatures in this forest?

Maverick: Ha ha, I hope not, Trippy. But if we do, we’ll just have to outsmart them.

Trippy: Outsmart them? How do you plan on doing that?

Maverick: Ha ha, I don’t know. But I’ll think of something. Maybe we can tell them a really bad joke and they’ll run away in terror.

Trippy: Ha ha, that’s a great idea, Maverick. Let’s give it a try.

Maverick: Okay, here goes. Hey, dangerous creature, do you know why horses are always so happy?

Dangerous creature: No, why?

Maverick: Because they always neigh-ver give up!

Dangerous creature: Ha ha, that was a terrible joke, Maverick. But I’m not scared of you. I’m the most dangerous creature in this forest, and you should be terrified of me.

Trippy: Ha ha, we’re not scared of you, dangerous creature. We’re brave and courageous, and we’ll take you on if we have to.

Maverick: That’s right, dangerous creature. We may be horses, but we’re not to be underestimated.

Dangerous creature: Ha ha, we’ll see about that. But for now, I’ll let you go. But watch out, because I’ll be watching you.

Trippy: Thanks, dangerous creature. We’ll be sure to keep an eye out for you.

Maverick: Let’s go, Trippy. Adventure awaits!
